About

ICON is an All India inter-collegiate cultural fest organised by the Computer Science Engineering department of Pondicherry Engineering College, one of the premier institutions of Puducherry. ICON is back with a bang this year with its stunning Disneyland theme. C'mon, take a look at our events list and you'll be amazed to know that there is something for everyone in both technical and non-technical categories. Roll out the coder, artist, photographer and the gamer within you. Mark the dates and be there at PEC auditorium on Feb 28th! We are sure you'd not regret being there.

TOPICS FOR PRESENTATION:

Note: You can select any topic of your choice in any domain. No domain restrictions.

Note: Send your abstract in softcopy to paperpresentationicon2k19@gmail.com

Please contact organisers for further details.

Date:Feb 28, 2019
Time:11:00 AM
Venue:
  • MCA -2nd yr classroom
  • MCA -3rd yr classroom
  • DCS M.Tech
  • IS M.Tech
  • Information Security Lab
Rules:
  1. Candidates should send their abstract in softcopy to paperpresentationicon2k19@gmail.com on or before 27.02.2019.
  2. Accepted papers will be intimated through their respective email id.
  3. Maximum of three participants are allowed for the presentation.
  4. Paper should be submitted as MS word and it should not exceed eight pages.
  5. Details of the team members: Name, Department, E-mail ID, Contact number, Institution name should be sent along with the paper.
  6. Bring your softcopies of the presentation in pendrive.
  7. Selected candidates should bring their identity card at the time of presentation.
  8. Selected candidates should bring three hard copies of IEEE format and softcopy of the presentation(powerpoint).
  9. The presentation time is for 6 mins and 2 mins for queries for each paper.

Please contact organisers for further details.
